## Overview

Setting up a VPN on Android is one of the easiest ways to protect your internet connection, hide your IP address, and browse more safely on mobile networks or public Wi-Fi.

In this tutorial, you will learn how to install a VPN app, add your VPN profile, connect to a server, and check if your VPN is working correctly.

## What You Need

Before starting, make sure you have:

1. An Android phone or tablet.
2. A working internet connection.
3. A VPN app installed.
4. A VPN account, subscription link, or server profile.

## Step 1: Install Your VPN App

Open the Google Play Store and search for your VPN app.

You can use apps like:

- Hiddify
- Clash Meta
- WireGuard
- OpenVPN Connect
- Your VPN provider’s official app

Install the app and open it.

![Install VPN app on Android](/uploads/android-vpn-install.jpg)

## Step 2: Add Your VPN Profile

Most VPN apps allow you to add a VPN profile using one of these methods:

- Subscription link
- QR code
- Config file
- Manual server details

If your provider gave you a subscription link, copy it first. Then open the VPN app and choose **Import from Clipboard** or **Add Subscription**.

## Step 3: Allow VPN Permission

Android will ask for VPN permission when you connect for the first time.

Tap **OK** or **Allow**.

This permission lets the VPN app create a secure tunnel for your internet traffic.

## Step 4: Choose a Server

After importing your profile, choose a server.

For best performance:

- Choose a nearby server for faster speed.
- Choose a low-ping server for browsing.
- Try different servers if one does not connect.
- Use a streaming server if you want video access.

## Step 5: Connect to VPN

Tap the **Connect** button.

When connected, you should see a VPN icon in the Android status bar.

![VPN connected on Android](/uploads/android-vpn-connected.jpg)

## Step 6: Test Your VPN

Open a browser and search for “what is my IP”.

If your IP address changed, your VPN is working.

You can also test:

- Website access
- App access
- Streaming speed
- Download speed

## Common Problems

### VPN does not connect

Try these fixes:

1. Change server.
2. Restart the VPN app.
3. Update your subscription.
4. Check your internet connection.
5. Restart your phone.

### Internet is slow

Try these fixes:

1. Select a closer server.
2. Use Wi-Fi instead of mobile data.
3. Disconnect and reconnect.
4. Test another protocol if available.

## Final Thoughts

Android VPN setup is simple once you understand the basic process:

1. Install the app.
2. Add your VPN profile.
3. Allow VPN permission.
4. Choose a server.
5. Connect and test.

After setup, you can use your Android device more safely on public Wi-Fi, mobile data, and unknown networks.
