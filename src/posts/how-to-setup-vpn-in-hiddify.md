## Overview

Hiddify is a modern VPN and proxy client that helps you connect to secure VPN servers using a subscription link, QR code, or manual configuration.

It supports many common proxy and VPN formats, including VLESS, VMess, Trojan, Reality, Hysteria, TUIC, WireGuard, SSH, Clash, and Sing-box profiles.

In this tutorial, you will learn how to set up a VPN profile in Hiddify step by step.

## What You Need Before Starting

Before you begin, make sure you have:

1. The Hiddify app installed on your device.
2. A valid VPN subscription link or QR code from your VPN provider.
3. A working internet connection.
4. Your VPN profile details if you are adding the server manually.

> Tip: If you received a subscription URL from your VPN provider, keep it copied before opening Hiddify.

## Step 1: Download and Install Hiddify

First, download the Hiddify app for your device.

Hiddify is available for multiple platforms, including:

* Android
* iOS
* Windows
* macOS
* Linux

Install the correct version for your device and open the app after installation.

![Download Hiddify app](/images/uploads/hiddify-download.jpg)

## Step 2: Open Hiddify

After installing the app, open Hiddify.

On the first launch, the app may ask for VPN permission or network permission. Allow the required permissions so Hiddify can create a secure VPN connection on your device.

On Android and iOS, you may see a system VPN permission popup. Tap **Allow** or **OK**.

## Step 3: Add Your VPN Profile

There are usually three common ways to add a VPN profile in Hiddify:

* Add subscription link
* Scan QR code
* Import from clipboard

The easiest method is using a subscription link.

### Method 1: Add Subscription Link

1. Copy your VPN subscription link.
2. Open Hiddify.
3. Tap the **+** button or **Add Profile** option.
4. Choose **Add from Clipboard** or **Import Subscription URL**.
5. Paste your subscription link.
6. Tap **Save** or **Import**.

Hiddify will load the available servers from your VPN subscription.

![Add subscription link in Hiddify](/images/uploads/hiddify-add-subscription.jpg)

### Method 2: Scan QR Code

If your VPN provider gives you a QR code:

1. Open Hiddify.
2. Tap **Add Profile**.
3. Choose **Scan QR Code**.
4. Allow camera permission if asked.
5. Scan the QR code.
6. Save the imported profile.

This method is useful when setting up VPN on mobile devices.

### Method 3: Import from Clipboard

If you copied a VLESS, VMess, Trojan, Clash, or Sing-box config:

1. Copy the full config text or link.
2. Open Hiddify.
3. Tap **Add Profile**.
4. Select **Import from Clipboard**.
5. Confirm the imported profile.

## Step 4: Update the Subscription

After adding your profile, update the subscription list.

1. Go to your imported profile.
2. Tap **Update** or **Refresh**.
3. Wait for Hiddify to load all available servers.
4. Check if server names appear in the list.

If no servers appear, your subscription link may be wrong, expired, or blocked by your network.

## Step 5: Select the Best Server

Once the server list appears, choose a server.

For better performance:

* Choose a nearby location for faster speed.
* Choose a low-ping server for browsing and gaming.
* Choose a streaming-friendly server if you want to watch geo-restricted content.
* Try another server if one server does not connect.

Some Hiddify versions include automatic or delay-based server selection. If available, use this feature to find the fastest server.

## Step 6: Connect to VPN

Now connect to your VPN:

1. Select your preferred server.
2. Tap the main **Connect** button.
3. Wait until the status changes to **Connected**.
4. Open your browser and test the connection.

On mobile devices, you should see a VPN icon in the status bar after connecting.

![Connected to VPN in Hiddify](/images/uploads/hiddify-connected.jpg)

## Step 7: Test Your VPN Connection

After connecting, test if your VPN is working.

You can check:

* Your IP address has changed.
* Websites load normally.
* Restricted websites or apps are accessible.
* Your connection speed is stable.

If the internet does not work after connecting, disconnect and try another server.

## Recommended Hiddify Settings

For most users, these settings are recommended:

### VPN Mode or TUN Mode

Use VPN/TUN mode if you want all apps on your device to use the VPN connection.

This is useful for:

* Browsers
* Messaging apps
* Streaming apps
* Games
* System-wide privacy

### Auto Update Subscription

Enable automatic subscription updates if your provider changes servers regularly.

This helps keep your server list fresh.

### Auto Select or Fastest Server

If your Hiddify app supports automatic server selection, enable it to connect to the fastest available server.

## Common Problems and Fixes

### Problem: Hiddify does not connect

Try these fixes:

1. Switch to another server.
2. Update the subscription.
3. Check your internet connection.
4. Restart the Hiddify app.
5. Restart your device.
6. Ask your VPN provider for a new subscription link.

### Problem: Internet stops working after connection

Try these fixes:

1. Disconnect and reconnect.
2. Choose another server.
3. Turn VPN/TUN mode off and on again.
4. Check DNS settings.
5. Update Hiddify to the latest version.

### Problem: Subscription link does not load

This can happen if:

* The link is expired.
* The link was copied incorrectly.
* Your VPN account has expired.
* Your network blocks the subscription domain.

Ask your VPN provider to resend the subscription link.

### Problem: Speed is slow

Try these steps:

1. Select a server closer to your location.
2. Use a low-ping server.
3. Avoid overloaded servers.
4. Test with Wi-Fi and mobile data.
5. Update the subscription and reconnect.

## Safety Tips

To use Hiddify safely:

* Only use subscription links from trusted providers.
* Do not share your subscription link publicly.
* Keep the app updated.
* Avoid unknown configuration files.
* Follow local laws and regulations when using VPN services.

## Final Thoughts

Hiddify is a powerful VPN and proxy client for users who want a flexible way to connect using subscription links, QR codes, or manual profiles.

For beginners, the easiest setup method is:

1. Install Hiddify.
2. Copy your subscription link.
3. Import it into Hiddify.
4. Update the profile.
5. Choose a server.
6. Tap Connect.

Once connected, your device traffic can route through the selected VPN server, helping you browse more securely and privately.
