## Overview

Using a VPN on Windows helps protect your internet traffic, hide your real IP address, and improve privacy when browsing, streaming, or working remotely.

This guide explains how to set up a VPN on Windows using a VPN app or manual configuration.

## What You Need

Before you start, prepare:

1. A Windows PC or laptop.
2. A VPN app or configuration file.
3. Your VPN username, password, or subscription link.
4. A stable internet connection.

## Method 1: Use a VPN App

The easiest method is to use your VPN provider’s app.

### Step 1: Download the VPN App

Go to your VPN provider’s website and download the Windows app.

Install the app like any other Windows program.

![Download VPN app for Windows](/uploads/windows-vpn-download.jpg)

### Step 2: Sign In or Import Profile

Open the app.

Depending on your VPN provider, you may need to:

- Sign in with your account.
- Paste a subscription link.
- Import a config file.
- Scan or copy server details.

### Step 3: Choose a Server

Choose a server based on your purpose:

- Nearby server for speed.
- Privacy-friendly location for general browsing.
- Streaming server for content access.
- Business server for remote work.

### Step 4: Connect

Click **Connect**.

Wait until the app shows **Connected**.

After connecting, your Windows traffic will route through the VPN.
